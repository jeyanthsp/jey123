Tandoori chicken

Tandoori chicken, a culinary gem hailing from the Indian subcontinent, is a dish that embodies the rich heritage and vibrant flavors of Indian cuisine. Marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices, including cumin, coriander, turmeric, and cayenne pepper, this chicken is then traditionally cooked in a tandoor, a clay oven that imparts a unique smoky flavor and charred aroma. The result is succulent, tender chicken with a tantalizing balance of spices, perfect for igniting the taste buds and transporting diners on a flavorful journey. Tandoori chicken is not just a dish; it's a celebration of tradition, craftsmanship, and the artistry of Indian culinary mastery.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • yogurt
  • oil
  • spices

Directions

  1. Mix Marinade: In a bowl, combine yogurt, minced garlic, minced ginger, lemon juice, salt, cayenne pepper, paprika, turmeric, cumin, and coriander.
  2. Coat Chicken: Add chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they're fully coated. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
  3. Grill/Bake: Grill chicken for 6-8 minutes per side or bake for 20-25 minutes, until cooked through and charred.

Tips

Add Lemon Juice: Lemon juice not only adds a bright, citrusy flavor to the marinade but also helps tenderize the chicken. Don't skip this ingredient!
